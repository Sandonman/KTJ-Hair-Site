Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 20, 2026

KTJ Hair values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website, submit a booking request, contact us, or choose to receive communications from us.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you interact with our website:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Appointment details
  • Service preferences and notes
  • Photos you choose to upload related to your hair or appointment request
  • Messages submitted through our contact form

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and manage appointment requests
  • Respond to questions and customer inquiries
  • Send booking confirmations, scheduling updates, and reminders
  • Maintain client records and service history
  • Improve our services and client experience

SMS Communications

If you choose to opt in, KTJ Hair may send you appointment-related text messages, including booking confirmations, reminders, scheduling updates, and replies to customer inquiries. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted service providers only as needed to operate our website and communications, such as scheduling, website hosting, email delivery, SMS delivery, and secure data storage providers.

Mobile information will not be shared, sold, or transferred to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information using secure tools and service providers. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You may contact us if you would like to update your information, request removal of your information where appropriate, or ask questions about how your data is used.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact KTJ Hair through the website contact form or by using the business contact information provided on the site.
