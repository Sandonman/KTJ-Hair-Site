# KTJ Hair Website Development

## Overview
Developed a website for a hairstylist business named KTJ Hair with features for booking services and contacting the stylist. The website includes sections for About, Gallery, Services, Booking, and Contact Me.

## Current Structure
- **Header:** Displays "KTJ Hair" title on the left, navigation menu at the top right.
- **Sections:**
  - **About:** Includes a placeholder image and description.
  - **Gallery:** Contains placeholders for images.
  - **Services:**
    - Multiple services with descriptions, prices, and time estimates. Each service has a corresponding image placeholder.
    - Services now have the option to add a haircut when applicable.
  - **Booking:**
    - Includes fields for client information and service selection, with a dropdown for the next 12 Thursdays for appointment dates and times from 9 AM to 5:00 PM in 30-minute increments. A note to upload pictures is also present.
  - **Contact Me:**
    - Includes fields for clients to provide their name, email, phone number, and a message box to reach out to the stylist.
